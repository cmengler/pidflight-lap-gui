Features

  • • Multiple pilot support
  • • Bluetooth, Ethernet, WiFi and USB
  • • Race countdown
  • • Lap time announcements
  • • Live race statistics

Hardware

Discover how to build your own lap timing device or bring your own existing compatible lap timing device to use with PIDflight Lap.

Visit the PIDflight Lap website for more information.

Support

PIDflight Lap firmware and cross-platform GUI is provided free of charge to all users.

If you find PIDflight Lap useful and would like to support continued development efforts please feel free to donate to this project or purchase the premium edition of the PIDflight Lap Android app, any donation or purchase is appreciated.
